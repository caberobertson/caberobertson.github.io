Finger Piano

Pull the strings with your fingers — computer vision hand tracking meets a physics-based plucked-string synth

📷 Camera Stage
Uses your webcam and MediaPipe Hand Landmarker (runs locally in the browser — no video is uploaded anywhere) to track your fingertips. Drag a fingertip sideways through a string to bend it, then lift off — it snaps back and rings out with real spring physics driving the animation and a Karplus-Strong plucked-string synth for the sound. Works best in good lighting, in Chrome or Edge.
